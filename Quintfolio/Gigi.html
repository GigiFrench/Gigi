About-The Artist





My portfolio demonstrates a multitude of varying mediums- ranging from clay to wood. Most of my work is hobbies I picked up whether that be from school, or boredom, and have utilized that creative freedom to create works of art. My favorite way of creating is painting on canvas and wood working. My drive to continue to create and aim for success is all in work to become an interior designer where I will get to create interior living spaces that reflect the space and the human.
