About-The Artist





Jonathan Fenton is a freshman at the New Jersey Institute of Technology in Newark, New Jersey. He is a member of both the Hillier College of Architecture and Design and the Albert Dorman Honors College. Majoring in the 5-year professional Bachelor of Architecture program, Jonathan hopes to complete a NAAB accredited degree and move ever closer to becoming a licensed architect.
