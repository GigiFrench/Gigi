Jordyn Fuchs















About The Artist





Jordyn Fuchs is a first year student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, and is curently enrolled in the five year B. Arch program. She is a member of the Hillier College of Architectur and Design as well as the Albert Dorman Honors College. Outside of her work in architecture, she's a long-time writer and visual artist, and has won awards for her achievements in both disciplines during high school.
